About

CountyThird EMS, founded in 2011, is a non-profit, volunteer EMS agency, best know for our innovative patient care solutions, and the outstanding treatment we provide to each and every member of our communities. Our team is currently comprised of 17 dedicated volunteers.

Located in the Atlanta Metro area, we've also served other agencies through our mutual aid contracts for over 10 years. Through our donations and grant programs, we support other non-profit organizations in our communities, including those helping empower students, assisting those in poverty, and continuing medical care.
