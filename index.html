About

CountyThird Medical, founded in 2012, is a non-profit "M.M.S.P.", best know for our innovative patient care technologies, and the outstanding treatment we provide to each and every member of our communities.

Located in Atlanta, we've served other agencies and NFPs for over 10 years. Through our donations and grants, we support organizations in our communities, including those helping empower students, and assisting those in poverty.
